## Miscellaneous

### Why *silveriron*?

- In Korean, **은 (Eun)** and **철 (Cheol)** are homonyms for **silver** ($^{47}\text{Ag}$) and **iron** ($^{26}\text{Fe}$), respectively. It's a chemical pun on my own name.
